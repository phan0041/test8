Life Story

Village

I was born in middle part of Vietnam and grew up in a small village. At that time, my parents were farmers and we lived in poverty but that was a happy time.

Vietnam War

The province where I was born is near border in Vietnam War and from young, I heard stories how my parents fought, how they hide from bombs during the war. There still have people died because of bombs left over on the field.

Divorce

My parents divorced. My mother brought me to a city in southern part of Vietnam.

Two years in primary three

It took me 2 years to finish primary 3. I learned a hard lesson a student from village who was awarded with credit in academic could not pass exam in city’s school. I realized that there is a GAP in education between village and city.

Physics

In secondary school I like physics and read many books about physics.
#followhobby

High school in Ho Chi Minh

I went to Ho Chi Minh city alone to study high school in a class streamline in Physics My Ho Chi Minh journey started.

National Physics Competition

Go Abroad

I “die-die” studied English to apply to my dream university in Singapore. Lucky God was with me. I got accepted. My Singapore journey started. #Let'sGoSingapore

Nanyang Technological University, Singapore

I started by an education in Computer Science with minor in business.
#Excited

Internship

During my six-month internship stint at Bank of America, I found that culture of financial industry is very professionally and nurturing. I greatly enjoyed the opportunities that were given to me. #tasteOfWorkingAdult

Join Citi after graduation

I was drawn to Citi because of its prestigious Technology Management Associate Program. Being in program equiped me with indepth business knowledge and the rotation structure gave me an exposure to different part of the bank.
